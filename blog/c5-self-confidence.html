Self Confidence

What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

They both aim to achieve the same thing. Mindfulness is a trained ability to focus attention, and in doing so bringing a calm restfulness and at once a degree of connection, but also detachment from your thoughts, emotions and environment. Self awareness is all about being conscious of, and being able to control, your thoughts and emotions, even in very stressful environments. Both lead towards increased emotional intelligence.
